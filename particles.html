Get to Know Us.

We are a robotics team that wants to see a world comprised of well-rounded innovators that understand the significance of S.T.E.A.M (Science, Technology, Engineering, Art, and Math). Each element of STEAM plays an essential role in society. We strive to educate and create the next generation of the global workforce. By implementing the principles of S.T.E.A.M., we hope to develop skills in students that will eventually be used to solve world issues.

Our Mission

Our mission is to raise the next generation of leaders by providing everyone in the community the chance to experience the fields of STEAM through robotics. Robotics gives students the chance to learn and utilize unique skill sets in various departments such as hardware, software, marketing, and business. Regardless of the subject of interest or specialization, there will always be an open space in robotics for it. We believe that everyone should be involved in creating a stronger, more connected community.

Our Outreach

Protocol X provides an educational environment that develops leadership skills, promotes innovation and creativity, and enhances both technical and nontechnical skills. We provide all the necessary tools and methods to improve skills such as design, assembly, and programming, as well as business, marketing, communication, and leadership. We promote S.T.E.A.M. growth in order to help students prepare for future careers with advantageous skills.

Not only do we create a positive impact within our team, but also within the local community. Some of our projects include teaching elementary students how to build and program using the LEGO Mindstorms kit, teaching underprivileged students how to use computers and basic programming, and hosting camps to promote the STEAM fields. Our mission is to create a stronger, connected community through robotics, and we enact that by giving back to the community.
